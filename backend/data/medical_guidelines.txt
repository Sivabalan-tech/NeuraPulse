
**NeuraPulse Medical Guidelines & Knowledge Base**

**1. General Health**
- Hydration: Adults should drink about 2-3 liters of water per day.
- Sleep: 7-9 hours of sleep is recommended for adults (18-64 years).
- Exercise: At least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous activity per week.

**2. Common Symptoms & Remedies**
- **Headache**: 
  - Causes: Dehydration, stress, eye strain, lack of sleep.
  - Remedies: Drink water, rest in a dark room, apply cold compress, take OTC pain reliever (Acetaminophen/Ibuprofen).
  - Warning: Seek help if sudden/severe (thunderclap), accompanied by stiff neck, confusion, or visual changes.

- **Cold & Flu**:
  - Symptoms: Runny nose, sore throat, cough, congestion, body aches.
  - Remedies: Rest, warm fluids, honey for cough, saline nasal spray.
  - Prevention: Wash hands, avoid close contact with sick individuals.

**3. First Aid Basics**
- **Burns**: Cool with running water for 20 mins. Cover with cling film/sterile dressing. Do NOT apply ice or butter.
- **Cuts/Scrapes**: Clean with water, apply antiseptic, cover with band-aid. Apply pressure if bleeding.
- **Choking**: Perform Heimlich maneuver (abdominal thrusts) if person cannot breathe/cough.

**4. Mental Health**
- Anxiety: Practice 4-7-8 breathing (inhale 4s, hold 7s, exhale 8s). Limit caffeine.
- Depression: Regular exercise, routine sleep, social connection. Professional therapy is recommended.
